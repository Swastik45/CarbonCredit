Getting Started

Welcome to CarbonCredit Marketplace! Our platform connects sustainable farmers with eco-conscious businesses to trade carbon credits.

Account Types

  • Farmers: Register plantations and earn carbon credits from sustainable farming practices.
  • Businesses: Purchase carbon credits to offset your company's emissions.

Registration Process

  1. Click "Get Started" on the homepage
  2. Choose your account type (Farmer or Business)
  3. Fill in your details and create a secure password
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile setup

Farmer Guide

Adding Plantations

To start earning carbon credits, you need to register your plantations:

  1. Log in to your farmer dashboard
  2. Navigate to "My Plantations"
  3. Click "Add New Plantation"
  4. Enter location coordinates (latitude/longitude)
  5. Specify tree type and plantation area
  6. Upload plantation images
  7. Submit for verification

NDVI Monitoring

Your plantations are monitored using satellite imagery and NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) analysis to ensure authenticity and calculate carbon sequestration.

Business Guide

Purchasing Carbon Credits

  1. Log in to your business dashboard
  2. Browse available carbon credits
  3. Select plantations that match your criteria
  4. Choose the amount of credits to purchase
  5. Complete the transaction
  6. Receive certification for your purchase

Verification Process

All plantations undergo a rigorous verification process:

  • Initial Review: Our team reviews submitted plantation data
  • Satellite Verification: NDVI analysis confirms vegetation health
  • Field Inspection: Random on-site visits for high-value plantations
  • Certification: Verified plantations receive official certification

Verification typically takes 7-14 business days.

Trading Carbon Credits

Pricing

Carbon credit prices are determined by:

  • Plantation size and tree type
  • NDVI scores and sequestration potential
  • Market demand and supply
  • Verification status

Transaction Security

All transactions are secured using blockchain technology, ensuring transparency and preventing fraud.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are carbon credits calculated?

Credits are calculated based on plantation area, tree type, and NDVI scores using scientifically validated formulas.

What types of trees are eligible?

We accept various tree species including eucalyptus, pine, oak, and native species. Contact us for specific requirements.

How long does verification take?

Standard verification takes 7-14 business days. Premium verification services are available for faster processing.

Are my transactions secure?

Yes, all transactions are secured using blockchain technology and undergo multiple verification steps.

Can I sell credits internationally?

Yes, our platform supports international trading with appropriate certifications for global compliance.
